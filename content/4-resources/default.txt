###Troubleshooting
Try to be patient when writing code, so much of programming is debugging – typos are one of the most common errors! If you find yourself stuck, try breaking your issue down into smaller, more manageable parts. 

Search Google or (link: http://stackoverflow.com/ text: Stack Overflow popup: yes) for how to solve those parts, one at a time. While frustrating, these bugs will may lead yout o discover something new you would not have considered before!
